The Challenge

The rules are simple.

7
01 / Marathons
Seven Marathons
Competitors must run seven full 42.2 km marathons. No shortcuts. Each leg is officially measured and certified.
7
02 / Days
Seven Consecutive Days
Complete every marathon within seven days — or 168 hours on the official race clock.
7
03 / Continents
Seven Continents
Antarctica, Africa, Australia, Asia, Europe, South America, and North America. One global circumnavigation.
Pushing beyond what you think is possible — that's where the real story lives. The 7·7·7 isn't a race. It's a redefinition.
Eric Koston Skateboarder · Pro Athlete
